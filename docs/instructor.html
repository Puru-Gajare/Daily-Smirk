Instructors

The Daily Smirk

is a revolutionary system for instructors to easily evaluate individual contributions to group projects.

Students rate each other every day they work on their project and recieve anonymized feedback from their group members.

In this way students can adjust their behavior immediately. Instructors can see this feedback instantly and assign grades based on contribution.

Dashboard displaying feedback for Leslie from her group members.

The dashboard displays information about each student in various views. Instructors can easily get a holistic sense of a team and how they view each other.
